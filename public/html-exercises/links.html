Top Picks: A Blog of my Bests and Greatests

My Top 3 Movie Picks

The third spot goes to Sister Act. It is about a Las Vegas Showgirl who was forced to hide in a convent and dress as a nun in order to hide from her mobster boyfriend. The movie is funny and the songs are catchy, and with Whoopi Goldberg's great acting, this definitely deserves the number 3 spot.

The second spot goes to Casablanca. Humphrey Bogart and Ingrid Bergman have a chemistry that glues your eyes to the screen. And with Casablanca being the archetype for modern unconditional love movies, I am proud that this is in the second spot.

And finally, the number one spot goes to Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2! Finally, the story of the boy who lived is coming to a close. The final battle between Voldemort and Harry launches this movie to the number 1 spot!

The Top 3 Songs of my Playlist

The third spot goes to Kids In Love. With Mayday Parade's vocals and emotional lyrics, this song makes you reminisce about your first love or that great love that you lost.

The second spot goes to Tenerife Sea. Ed Sheeran wows us again with his amazing vocals and lyrics. This song will make you think about that special someone that just takes your breath away.

And finally, the top spot goes to Babaylan! This song will make you go back to your roots and just march outside while chanting this: Magbabangon ang mga Babaylan!
