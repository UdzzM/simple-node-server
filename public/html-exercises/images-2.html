Harry Potter Boy Muggle


I was actually Harry Potter when I was a child. But when my Hogwarts letter didn't come in the mail via Hedwig, my Harry Potter Boy Wizard look faded and I ended up looking more and more a muggle as I grew older.











As I continued living in the muggle world, I was forced to enroll in a muggle school because my Hogwarts letter apparently got lost along with Hedwig. I decided to engage in sports instead and won the Basketball Championships along with my teammates (though it would have been better if the Quidditch Championship Cup was what I was holding)











After years of waiting, my letter finally came! Though it may not be from Hogwarts, it's the next best thing here in the muggle world - a letter from UP stating that I have passed the dreaded UPCAT! 3 years after I received that letter, here I am now, spreading unicorn poop in UPLB and turning beer into water. (and occasionally irritating my dog, Bart)











Hopefully, after a few years, I can transform myself into a duck and swim in pools of treasure. Kidding aside, I do hope that I be known in my field which is writing and make loads of money along the way. Because, let's be honest, money can buy me happiness. It's hard to cry while inside a Bugatti Veyron or Lamborghini Egoista.
