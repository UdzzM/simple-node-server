The Ebay of Firearms

Aside from drugs, guns are one of the most popular items on the dark web. From pistols and rifles to grenades and ammo, the dark web has it all. One of the most famous site on the dark web is The Armory. Sam Matthews managed to have a Q&A with The Armory last 2014 and some of the response were quite frank. The Armory states that they outsource their guns from the USA and from Military Reserves. They ship the ordered items inside power tools, tanks, engines, furnitures, to name a few. Their technique is that they find items with the similar density to the metal of the guns so as to by-pass X-rays at customs. Unlike other arms dealer, The Armory supposedly do not sell firearms to everyone. Here is a shortlist of those they do not transact with:

Hitmen Services

I will ‘neutralize’ the ex you hate, your bully, a policeman that you have been in trouble with, a lawyer, a small politician… I do not care what the cause is. I will solve the problem for you. Unfriendly Solutions goes right down to business, when it comes to hitman services.

Hiring a contract killer was thought to be difficult as it requires you to have certain connections to get a hitman. But with the birth of the web, it's now as easy as clicking a button... in the comfort of your own home. The dark web, which is home to almost every illegal service there is, is no stranger to hitman services. With various websites like Unfriendly Solutions, Hitman Network and Ender Vida offering their service. There are several modes of hiring a hitman. In the Hitman Service, you 'purchase' their service first and they will then contact you within 1-2 days. In Ender Vida, it's quite different. You message them with a subject saying 'Hit me' and nothing else, they will then send you a secure email address and password for communication. Their modes of payment are done in Bitcoins, the standard currency of the dark web.
