The Silk Road of the Modern Internet

The Silk Road of the dark net works like the Silk Road of the older times, except for one striking difference, the Silk Road of the dark net sells mostly illegal stuff and its best-selling item are drugs. Drugs are no stranger to the dark web, with different sites offering different goods with varying place of origin, purity and effectiveness. Unlike other illegal services oon the dark web, a lot of websites are dedicated to the dealing of drugs. Though some are of questionable reliability, with their goods laced up with other substances, some are trustworthy, with their MDMAs being 85% pure and no hint of any other active ingredient. The prices are usually lower than what your local drug dealer offers, and when it comes to security, buying drugs on the dark web are a bit safer as they have private shipping and are paid in Bitcoins, the currency of the dark web.
